Services offered

The Reception Area

Our reception area has many purposes. We can accomodate up to 150 people for a reception or other type of gathering. Tables, chairs and other amenities are provided. Set-up and cleaning is included in the cost. Please Contact Us with specific questions about hosting a reception.


Meal options include:

  • Brunch
  • Lunch
  • Dinner

Full-Time Temple Missionaries


We provide long term lodging for full-time missionary couples. Our suites include a full bathroom, living room with television, kitchen and a bedroom with an extra set of bunk-beds for any short term visitors.
